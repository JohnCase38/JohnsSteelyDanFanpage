Facts about the band:

  • Formed in 1971 at Bard College in New York by classmates Walter Becker and Donald Fagen, they quickly moved to Los Angeles to begin their journey as a band
  • The two have recorded 9 studio albums along with several live albums featuring a number of session musicians
  • Walter played guitars, bass, and sang backup vocals while Donald plays the keys and sang lead vocals for most of their albums
  • They have sold over 40 million records and were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2001
